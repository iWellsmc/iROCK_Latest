Guidelines For Invoice Upload On iROCK Software

The Guidelines For Invoice Upload and Registration Will be as Follows:

Invoice Upload is to be done only by the assigned Primary and Secondary Users of the Vendor Company that are registered in the iROCK software for {{company.company_name}}.

Vendor shall communicate to {{company.company_name}} for the replacement of any of the above-mentioned registered users.

Invoicing Procedure:

Step 1: Vendor must fill up the Standard Information Form and Checklist on the iROCK software.

Each invoice shall clearly state the following:

• Invoice Number

• Invoice Date

• Period of Service

• Relevant Contract/Amendment/Variation Order Name

• Relevant Contract/Amendment/Variation Order Number

• Type of Service (Service, Supply or Services and Supply)

• Brief Description of Service Rendered

• Quantities of Material Delivered

• Location of Service (Installation site, Rig etc.)

• Project Name

• Block Name

• Field Name

• Pad/Platform/Cluster Name

• Facility Name

• Well name

• Currency and Currency Split where applicable.

• Gross Amount

• Taxes

• Discounts where applicable

• Net Amount Payable

• Payment terms

• Bank Account Details




Step 2: Vendor shall submit and upload the invoices along with the following:

• Call Off or Instructions.

• Mobilization Notices.

• Work Completion Certificates.

• Material Delivery Tickets/Certificates

• Timesheets.

• Third Party Invoices.

• Contracts/Amendments/Variation Orders.

• Price Template for attached Contracts/Amendments/Variation Orders.

• Other Supporting Documents necessary to verify the correctness of the items invoiced.


All the above listed documents must be duly signed by {{company.company_name}} Representative as this will be the only measure for acceptance for all supporting documents provided.

The uploading function will not work until Step 1 is fully completed.

Notes:

1. The invoice must be billed and addressed to the Client Company as stated in the relevant awarded contract.

2. Vendor must ensure that invoices are sent as per contractual terms immediately goods are delivered, or services rendered. Invoices not sent to {{company.company_name}} as per contractual terms after job execution will not be accepted.

3. For awarded contracts which have a currency split for the services or supply, Vendor is required to:

• Submit two separate invoices reflecting the currency split accordingly.

• The invoice number must show clear reference to the currency split in the following manner:

Invoice Part A - represents the higher percentage of currency split.

Invoice Part B - represents the lower percentage of currency split.

4. When an invoice has incomplete information or does not meet up to the above listed requirements, {{company.company_name}} will return the invoice for correction and the revised invoices shall be Submitted back within {{processdate}} working days for invoice processing.

5. When re-submitting an Invoice, Vendor must do the following:

• Ensure that the reSubmitted invoice shows reference to the previous invoice rejected.

• The previous invoice rejected must be included with the other supporting documents when re-submitting the revised invoice.
